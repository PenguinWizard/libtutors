About Us

Learn about the program

What We Do

We provide free tutoring services for young kids (pre-k to 8th grade) in the Cambrian Branch Library. We offer a variety of subjects, from math to english, and even science. Our organization is comprised solely of highschoolers.

Why Choose Us

With our services, you can get your child tutored for absolutely free, and we can accomodate for erradic schedules. Additionally, since our tutors are in highschool, they can effectively connect and relate with the kids they tutor.

How it Works

Go to the Signups page of this website, and fill out a quick form. We will send an email to you, and from there sessions can be planned. On the day of your tutoring sessions, simply go to the library and we will be waiting for you in one of the study rooms (we will specify via email which study room will be used).

Our Mission

We aim to give everyone the amazing gift of tutoring by making our services as accesible as possible. Our mission is to help prepare the next generation of learners, so they can suceed in education.

FAQ

Bring any textbook/worksheet you have with the problems/questions you need help with. Our tutors will supply whiteboards, papers, and writing utensils. Bring a computer if needed.

Our subjects are divided into math, history, english, and science. Math includes any mathematical concepts taught from elementary to 8th grade, such as solving equations, etc. History includes content on global history (1200 and forward). English can include literary analysis, reading, and writing help. Finally, science includes coding, chemistry, physics, biology, etc.

Sessions are held in the Cambrian Branch Library study rooms. The specific study room you will go to will be specified via an email we send you.

To cancel a session, simply email us. We would appreciate informing us on your cancelation at least 24 hours in advance.
