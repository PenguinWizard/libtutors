About Us

Learn about the program

We offer free and unlimited tutoring services for young kids (elementary and middle school students) at the Cambrian Branch Public Library, as well as online. We offer help in a variety of subjects such as English, math, science, and history. Each specific subject has specialized tutors for optimal and productive tutoring.

Our organization provides tutoring for a wide range of subjects. All our tutors are currently taking part in quite rigorous courses, such as AP Calculus and AP World History, and are students who can connect more with who they are tutoring. Moreover, we tutor for FREE, both in person and online (through zoom meetings).

Sign up for a tutoring session via the “Signups” page on this website. Choose your prefered subjects, include any comments, and that's it! We will confirm your session by sending out an email or text message two days prior to your scheduled timing.
